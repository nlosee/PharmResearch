# Pharma Regulatory Pulse — 2026-06-08

## Regulatory Spotlight
**Achieve Life Sciences’ cytisinicline NDA is approaching its PDUFA target action date**
Achieve Life Sciences reported that FDA accepted its NDA for cytisinicline for nicotine dependence/smoking cessation, with a PDUFA target action date of June 20, 2026. The submission was supported by two completed Phase 3 studies and an open-label safety study; the company also noted a completed Phase 2 vaping-cessation study and a successful end-of-Phase 2 meeting with FDA for a future vaping indication. [Source 39]

## Renal and Liver Disease
### Clinical Trials

**United Therapeutics advances UKidney xenotransplantation into an ESRD study**
FDA cleared United Therapeutics’ IND for the UKidney xenotransplantation clinical trial. The study will enroll an initial cohort of six end-stage renal disease patients, expanding to up to 50 participants, and is intended to support a BLA. [Source 21]
https://ir.unither.com/press-releases/2025/02-03-2025-120011819

## Infectious Disease
### Clinical Trials
**FDA Clears Phase IIa RSV Program for ASC10**
Ascletis Pharma received FDA approval to conduct a Phase IIa trial of ASC10 in mild to moderate RSV infection. The placebo-controlled, double-blind, randomized study will assess tolerability, pharmacokinetics, antiviral activity, and safety. [Source 16]
https://www.clinicaltrialsarena.com/news/ascletis-rsv-infection-therapy
