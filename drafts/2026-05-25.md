# Pharma Regulatory Pulse — 2026-05-25

## Regulatory Spotlight
**FDA Approves First-in-Class Non-Opioid Analgesic for Moderate to Severe Acute Pain**
FDA approved Journavx (suzetrigine) 50 mg oral tablets for moderate to severe acute pain in adults, identifying it as the first drug in a new non-opioid analgesic class. The agency also reiterated its broader support for non-opioid pain development through the FDA Overdose Prevention Framework and its draft guidance on non-opioid analgesics for acute pain. [Source 1][Source 2]
https://www.fda.gov/news-events/press-announcements/fda-approves-novel-non-opioid-treatment-moderate-severe-acute-pain
