# Pharma Regulatory Pulse — 2026-04-27

## Regulatory Spotlight
**FDA Issues Draft Guidance to Expand Non-Opioid Options for Chronic Pain**
FDA issued draft guidance, *Development of Non-Opioid Analgesics for Chronic Pain*, to accelerate safe and effective non-opioid treatments and to reduce prescription-related opioid misuse. The guidance emphasizes trial design, appropriate patient populations, and meaningful outcomes including reduction in opioid use, and it was issued to fulfill Section 3001(b) of the SUPPORT Act. [Source 1]
https://www.fda.gov/news-events/press-announcements/fda-issues-new-guidance-expand-non-opioid-options-chronic-pain-curb-misuse
